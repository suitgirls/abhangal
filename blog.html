La'Naia J. Jones

Arya Bhangale / July 23rd, 2024

La’Naia J. Jones is Billington CyberSecurity’s Chief Information Officer and Director of the Information Enterprise. She also served as the National Security Agency’s Deputy CIO and as their Information Sharing and Safeguarding Executive. She worked to optimize the NSA’s IT strategy for the CIO Inspector General and Policy. She has over 19 years of leadership and technical experience in the NSA across multiple mission areas, she worked to manage IT investments and acquisition programs. She has also led modernization efforts to transform the Information Technology Enterprise through the utilization of the cloud, data sharing, as well as cybersecurity.

She also served 4 years in Tokyo as she was deployed in several different war zones. She also visited some of the United States Governments global operating locations. And while she was there she provided her technical expertise and background to ensure the security and protection of the IT landscape. During her time in Tokyo she also designed different infrastructures that provided abilities to collaborate to strengthen foreign relationships and enhance domestic partnerships through the resolution of IT issues of technological organizations.

She has a dual Bachelors of Science in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Maryland Eastern Shore. She also has a Master of Science in Technology Management from the University of Maryland, Global Campus. She has lended her endless knowledge and experience to the CIA to ensure that they harness the fast evolving digital technology to help strengthening key partnerships to advance our mission.
